My research develops mathematical theory for analysis and control of partial differential equations. The main focus is on deep and detailed analysis of challenging models arising from real-world applications.

Welcome!

I am a Professor of Mathematics and the leader of the Systems Theory Research Group at Tampere University, Finland. I am also the current leader of the Mathematics Research Centre at Tampere University.

My research focuses on the following topics:
  • Functional analysis
  • Mathematical systems theory
  • Analysis of partial differential equations
