Open Positions in Semigroup Theory and Functional Analysis

This list of open positions related to analysis of strongly continuous semigroups of operators, evolution equations, and related topics. The list is maintained by Lassi Paunonen (Tampere University, Finland)

To add details of a new position, send the information in the standard form to Lassi Paunonen by email (lassi dot paunonen [at] tuni dot fi). You can also sign up for a mailing list to receive information whenever a new position is added by sending an email to the same address.
